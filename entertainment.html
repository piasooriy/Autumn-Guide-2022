What's up Oslo

- Fall 2022

Octoberfest
Experience oktoberfest in Oslo! Be sure to come thirsty and hungry for beer and bavarian dishes!
Faun/Boléro/Canvas
Experience three ballett plays in one show, with legendary music from Ravel, Debussy and Chopin
Jane Eyre
Watch the masterpiece written by Charlotte Brönte come to life at the national theatre. This play is an aesthetic explotion filled with drama, emotional rollercoaster and dark secrets
Open house
Are you curious about the architecture in Oslo? Open house is a concept which invites everyone to architectual masterpieces in Oslo and Akershus, being buildings, interior, gardens, parks and conservatories
Salt
A place where you can try traditional sauna before you plunge in the ocean, while also enjoying stand-up, poetry and musical entertainment with a drink in your hand and plenty of food to choose from
Elvelangs
Experience 4,500 torches and 160 different entertainments along the famous Aker-river, which runs through Oslo.
